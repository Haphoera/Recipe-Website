Bunny Cookies

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of Cooking Butter
  • 1 cup of Castor Sugar
  • 3 cups of Self-Raising Flour
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp of Baking Powder

STEPS

-Preheat oven to 180 degrees with fan.Line two baking trays with baking paper.

-With mixer, combine butter and sugar until creamy, then mix in egg.

-In a dry bowl whisk baking powder and flour together, then add a little at a time to the mixer.

Lightly flour the bench and roll out the dough. Use cookie cutters to cut out shapes.

-Place cookies on the baking tray and cook for 6-8 minutes.

-Remove from oven and cool or 10 minutes.

Icing

  • 50g of butter (1/2 cup)
  • 1 cup of icing sugar
  • 1 tbsp of boiling water
  • 1 drop of food colouring

-With mixer combine butter, icing sugar and water.

-Add foor colouring.

-Spread over cooled cookies.
